Our History

Our Services

We provide a variety of reliable installation and maintenance services of electrical equipments.

Installation and maintenance of Transformers

We do installation maintenance of Transformers from 50KVA to 2500KVA. We also do installation of Switchgears and protection relays.

Installation and maintenance of Standby Generators

We do maintenance and installation of generators from 100KVA to 2.5MVA. We also do hire generators out and install and maintain change over switches.

Installation of Low Voltage Panels

We do installation of LV panels, street lights, circuit breakers and UPS system.

Cable Installation and Fault Finding

We do cable installation,cable fault finding and location, cable termination and joints. We also install mini-subs.
