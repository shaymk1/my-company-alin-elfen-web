Our History

Alin Elfen Electrical Enterprises

Alin Elfen Electrical Enterprises is level 2 BEE contributor. It was established in 1998 under the leadership of two qualified women. We have grown steadily over the past 20 years to become one of the most fierce competitor within the electrical high voltage, medium voltage and generators maintenance and installation fields.
